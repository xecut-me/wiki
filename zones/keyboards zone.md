# Keyboards Zone

The Keyboard Zone is a place to leave your leftover keyboard switches for others to use.

We have a variety of switches, keycaps, switch testers, and other keyboard accessories, including rubber feet.
From time to time, you'll also find different keyboard models here to try out.

## Cantor MX

We usually keep all the essential parts for building a CantorMX in stock, including PCBs, controllers, and TRRS jacks.

You can build your own keyboard, just bring your favorite switches, keycaps, and a TRRS-to-TRRS cable. We'll help you with firmware flashing and initial setup.
